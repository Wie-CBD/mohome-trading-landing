About Us

We are a trading company that was founded in 1997 specializing in supplying aluminum alloy and non-alloy materials to manufacturing companies. We have an extensive network of aluminum producers and distribute to our clients around the globe.

Our Service

Our service is to guarantee the quality of goods according to orders and deliver them to our clients on time. To provide the best value and quality products and ensuring every customer is very satisfied through superior service and innovation
